Notes about trees: $T$ a tree and $f\not\in T$, then $T\cup\{f\}$ has a unique cycle $C_T(f)$

Fix a spanning tree $T$ of $G$

Fact: Every cycle $C\subseteq G$ is a combination of cycles $C_T(f)$

Cycles of the form $C_T(f)$ form a cycle basis

Rewrite above in terms of $T$:

$\small{\displaystyle\sum_{e\in T}(m_e-m_f)(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})=0}$
