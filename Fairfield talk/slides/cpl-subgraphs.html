Slope Relations

Let $G$ be a graph

Get tree polynomial $\tau_H$ for each $H\subseteq G$ with coupled spanning trees

Slope relations are given by all tree polynomials of all subgraphs with coupled spanning trees

For the algebraists:

$I=(\tau_H~|~H\subseteq G~\textrm{has coupled spanning trees})$ $\phantom{I}\subseteq k[m_e~|~e\in G]$
