Slope=Length

Theorem: $G$ is slope rigid if and only if it is length rigid


"Proof": verties at $(x_1,y_1)$ and $(x_2,y_2)$

distance between vertices $\sqrt{(y_1-y_2)^2+(x_1-x_2)^2}$

slope of line through vertices $\displaystyle\frac{y_1-y_2}{x_1-x_2}$

Essentially the same information $~~~~~\blacksquare$
