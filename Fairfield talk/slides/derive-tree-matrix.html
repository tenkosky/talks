Slope Relations

Let $C$ be a cycle in $G$ and add the lines in point-slope form

$\small{\displaystyle\sum_{e\in C}y_{e_1}-y_{e_2}=\sum_{e\in C}m_{e}(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})}$


$\small{\displaystyle\phantom{\sum_{e\in C}y_{e_1}-y} 0=\sum_{e\in C}m_{e}(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})}$


We eliminated the $y$-coordinates

Also: $\small{\displaystyle\sum_{e\in C}(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})=0}$

Fix an edge $f\in C$ and combine above:

$\small{\displaystyle\sum_{e\in C\setminus\{f\}}m_e(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})-m_f\sum_{e\in C\setminus\{f\}}(x_{e_1}-x_{e_2})=0}$
